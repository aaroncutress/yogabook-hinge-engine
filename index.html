Calibration Guide

To accurately map your sensors, the system needs to measure two physical anchors.

  1. Flat (180°): Open the laptop completely flat on a table.
  2. Tablet (360°): Fold the screen all the way back.

⚠️ Ensure your volume is up!

Audio beeps will tell you when to move the lid and when to hold it steady.
